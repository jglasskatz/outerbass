Outer Bass 2026: INTO THE ABYSS

A two-night descent into music, connection, and the strange beauty that lives just below the surface.

Your expedition pass includes:

Two full days of programming

Live bands, DJs, art, yoga, breathwork, ecstatic dance, and unexpected moments in between. Sunlit folk and funk drifting through the trees... giving way to late-night bassssss reverberating through the forest floor.

Two nights of camping

Settle into a beautiful redwood forest + meadow venue in the Santa Cruz Mountains. Pitch your tent, wander between stages, and yes... there are showers (Oooooh, ahhhhhhhh).

A pool (!!)

A small but mighty oasis for daytime hangs, cooling off, and spontaneous joy.

Two communal dinners (included)

That's right — dinner is covered both nights. Come hungry, leave nourished. (Breakfast + lunch are on your own — plan accordingly or bring snacks to share.)

Multiple stages, evolving environments

From daytime grove gatherings to a transformed "midnight zone" after dark — each space has its own current to follow.

Lodging & Arrival Notes:

Standard Camping (included)

Plenty of space to set up your tent and make a cozy home for the weekend.

Glamping Tents (optional add-on)

A softer landing — pre-set, more spacious, and tucked into a quieter, more tranquil pocket of the site.

Carpooling encouraged

Parking is limited, so we strongly encourage sharing rides where possible. Please purchase a Docking Pass if you anticipate driving your car, boat, submarine, etc. to the event.

Outer Bass is a not-for-profit gathering, built by a community of friends, artists, and curious humans who believe in creating something special together. Your ticket supports the artists, the land, and the experience itself — nothing more, nothing less.
